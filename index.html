Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AI Therapist.

Is AI Therapist a replacement for a human therapist?

AI Therapist is not intended to replace professional mental health treatment. It's designed to provide support, coping strategies, and a space for reflection. For severe mental health concerns, diagnosed conditions, or crisis situations, we recommend seeking help from licensed mental health professionals. Some users use AI Therapist as a supplement to traditional therapy, while others use it as an accessible first step toward mental wellness.

How private are my conversations?

Your privacy is our top priority. All conversations are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your personal data with third parties. You can delete your conversation history at any time. Our AI is trained on anonymized data and continuously improved with privacy-preserving techniques.

What therapeutic approaches does AI Therapist use?

AI Therapist incorporates elements from evidence-based therapeutic approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction, and Solution-Focused Brief Therapy. The system adapts its approach based on your needs and preferences.

Can I use AI Therapist in a crisis?

AI Therapist can provide coping strategies for managing difficult emotions, but it is not designed for crisis intervention. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency or having thoughts of harming yourself or others, please contact your local emergency services, call a crisis hotline like the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 988, or go to your nearest emergency room.

Do I need internet access to use AI Therapist?

Yes, AI Therapist requires an internet connection to function. However, we've designed our mobile app to have some limited offline capabilities, allowing you to access previously saved exercises and resources when you're without internet access.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term commitments or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to AI Therapist until the end of your current billing period.
